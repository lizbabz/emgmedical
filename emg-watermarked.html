What we offer

Comprehensive care
for every stage of life

🩺
Family Medicine

Ongoing care for patients of all ages. Establish your family's medical home with one of our experienced physicians.

🚶
Walk-In Care

No appointment needed for illnesses, minor injuries, infections, and urgent non-emergency concerns.

👩‍⚕️
Women's Health

Pap tests, maternity care, prenatal support, and specialist gynecology referrals on-site.

📋
Medical Forms

Driver's medicals, sick notes, WCB claims, insurance forms, pre-employment physicals, and more.

💉
Immunizations

Flu vaccines, hepatitis and tetanus injections, travel immunizations, and routine vaccinations.

🧓
Senior & Child Care

Dedicated care programs for seniors and children, including residence forms and allergy management.

🔬
Minor Procedures

Biopsies, minor surgical procedures, and allergy injections performed in-clinic by our physicians.

💊
Pharmacy

On-site pharmacy services at select locations for convenient prescription filling after your visit.
